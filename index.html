We'll Reach Our Goal

The Chronic Fatigue Syndrome Foundation, is to serve as the major resource for the treatment of ME-CFS, research, and education. We are committed to provide ready access, for the one who carries the syndrome and those close to the patients who get to experince the debilitating diseases. We will provide education to patients and their families, health professionals, the public, and corporate and government to make educated decisions when dealing with CFS patients, and to help overcome the stigma about CFS and better ensure support for broader availability and better access to needed treatment. We will be a leader in research to discover the underlying cause of Chronic Fatigue Syndrome, and will use the knowledge we gain to help develop new and more effective treatments.
